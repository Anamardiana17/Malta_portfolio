# Therapist Segmentation v2 — Structural Management Read

This layer is designed as a structural management lens supporting Malta portfolio storytelling across 2017-01 to 2025-12.
It should be read as a stable therapist management segmentation layer, not as a raw monthly performance fact table.

## Portfolio-level distribution
- premium anchor: 26 therapists (30.6%)
- throughput stabilizer: 10 therapists (11.8%)
- commercial builder: 21 therapists (24.7%)
- balanced core: 4 therapists (4.7%)
- coaching priority: 24 therapists (28.2%)

## Management interpretation
- Premium anchor therapists form the flagship-quality core and support premium service credibility.
- Throughput stabilizers support operating continuity and day-to-day execution stability.
- Commercial builders represent development-ready capacity with upside in conversion and guest retention.
- Coaching priority therapists indicate where structured management attention is still needed.
- Balanced core therapists remain useful as a stable contributor base but are not yet the defining story.
