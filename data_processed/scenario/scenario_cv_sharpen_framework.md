### 9. What to Avoid in the CV
- Avoid sounding like a pure analyst
- Avoid overusing framework-heavy language without action words
- Avoid generic task descriptions without management outcomes
- Avoid presenting the project as a dashboard exercise only
- Avoid weakening managerial tone with overly technical wording

---

### 10. Recommended Tone
The CV should sound:
- decisive
- commercially aware
- operationally grounded
- service-quality conscious
- managerially credible

It should not sound:
- overly academic
- overly technical
- purely consultative
- vague about execution

---

### 11. Best Positioning Line
This portfolio should support the message that I am a Spa Manager with analytical discipline, not an analyst looking for a management use case.
