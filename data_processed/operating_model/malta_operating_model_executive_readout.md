# Malta Operating Model — Executive Readout

This document integrates the staffing management layer, therapist structural segmentation layer, and manager action queue into a single portfolio-facing operating read.
It is designed as an executive operating-model narrative for the Malta portfolio across 2017-01 to 2025-12, rather than as a raw monthly fact table.

## Portfolio-level management logic
- The staffing layer explains whether each outlet holds coverage buffer, balanced staffing, or emerging people-risk pressure.
- The therapist layer explains the structural people mix across premium anchors, throughput stabilizers, commercial builders, balanced core, and coaching-priority therapists.
- The action-queue layer converts those readings into clear managerial priorities.
