CARISMA SPA & WELLNESS
SOPHISTICATED, DILIGENT AND PERSONAL PAMPERING

SOPHISTICATED, DILIGENT AND PERSONAL PAMPERING

Step into a world of wellness excellence at Carisma Spa & Wellness, Malta’s premier spa chain. Globally renowned and celebrated for our award-winning excellence in massages, signature Turkish Bath (Hammam), and holistic wellness services, including our Halo therapy room, Carisma Spa offers a sanctuary for rejuvenation, harmonizing the body, mind, and soul.

Experience the transformative power of our Hammam, where the over 2000 years old tradition of a cleansing ritual unfolds amidst warm waters, detoxifying and invigorating on both emotional and physical levels. Our exclusive Hammam purifying rituals and top-tier face and body treatments are meticulously crafted to elevate your well-being, exceeding expectations at every turn.

In the spirit of comprehensive care and celebration, our services extend to cater special occasions, including personalized experiences for bachelor and hens parties. Ensuring a day of relaxation and pampering, these tailored packages allow groups to enjoy our spa facilities, offering a blend of signature massages, osteopathic and therapeutic treatments and celebratory indulgences: Birthdays, anniversaries,  romantic surprises, team-building events, personalised wellness pampering plan – holiday journeys: single, couple and group packages.

As a token of appreciation, our esteemed hotel guests receive a Pre-Booking Gift, enhancing and complimenting their chosen spa treatment experience. Looking for surprises or perfect gifts? Explore our customizable spa gift cards, ideal for any occasion, from birthdays to holidays, including thoughtful pre – wedding gifts that ensure a truly personalized spa day experience for you and your loved ones.

With over three decades of dedication to excellence, Carisma Spa sets the standard for beauty and wellness. Our handpicked therapists from around the world deliver unparalleled treatments across hotels in Malta, ensuring every moment with us is extraordinary.

What sets Carisma apart in the realm of excellent customer service is our dedicated live web chat Spa advisor and assistant, always available to answer all your questions.

Discover serenity and embark on a journey of wellness and luxury at Carisma Spa & Wellness.
