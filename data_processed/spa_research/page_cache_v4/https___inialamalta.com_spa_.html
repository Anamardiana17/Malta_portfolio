spa Information

Relaxation and Rejuvenation

Stepping into the spa, situated in the heart of the historic hotel’s stone vaults, creates a sense of escape and tranquillity. Aesthetic beauty combined with bespoke spa experiences encourages the restoration of balance to the body, mind and soul – an integral part in one’s wellness journey.  Spa facilities include double and single treatment rooms, sauna, relaxation area, and a heated pool. Private showers and changing room facilities are also available.

Your Wellbeing Journey

Your wellbeing journey starts with a consultation where we understand your overall needs. We will select the best mix of therapies for you, paired with signature aromatherapy blends and some of the most exclusive beauty brands available. Our expert therapists will design a tailored wellness journey to improve your long-term holistic wellbeing.

Body Experiences

Experience inner peace and physical wellbeing. Using unique, signature blends of ingredients and a fusion of holistic therapies, our body experiences relieve tension and uplift your spirits.

Ayurveda Healing

Ayurveda treatments are all natural ancient treatments which are centred around the connection of the body, mind and spirit. The combination of personalised oil blends and Ayurvedic practices improve wellness and encourage self-healing which will leave you feeling rebalanced and renewed.

Spa Rituals

Experiences that focus on the mind, body and spirit. Easing through your senses, they will leave you feeling restored and complete.

Hands & Feet

Treat yourself to beautiful hands and nails, or rejuvenate your feet with our exclusive range of restorative, anti-ageing treatments.
